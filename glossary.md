# Glossary

## People

**B.B. Beauchamp (Bartholomew Byron Beauchamp)** - Tale collector who publishes vignettes in city broadsheets. Travels to remote regions interviewing people, then embellishes their stories.

**Jorin** - Squire to Sir Bram of Thornwick. Died in the ghoul ambush beneath Dunmorrow.

**King Edmund** - Current monarch. Events are dated by his regnal years.

**Sir Bram of Thornwick** - Paladin who served at the Chapel of Saint Aldara. Willem's master.

**Sir Willem, Breaker of the Dark Host** - Legendary paladin. As a boy of thirteen, served as torchbearer to Sir Bram. Witnessed an angelic miracle in the catacombs beneath Dunmorrow.

## Places

**Dunmorrow** - Small town south of Rainsend. Site of the ghoul infestation in the old catacombs.

**Rainsend** - Northern port city. Lumber trade, working people. Dreary, wet climate.

**Solmara** - Major southern city on the west coast. Cultural center with printing presses. Named for the sun but known for bone-chilling damp winters.

**Thornwick** - Town where Sir Bram was based. Location of the Chapel of Saint Aldara.

## Religion

**The One God** - Monotheistic god of order and light worshipped throughout the realm. Manifests through saints.

**Saint Aldara of the Lantern** - Patron of those who venture into dark places. Her symbol is the lantern-and-star. Angels of her order carry lanterns with shadowless flame.

**The Lantern** - Symbol and aspect of Saint Aldara. Represents light carried into darkness.

## Organizations

**Chapel of Saint Aldara** - Chapel in Thornwick where Sir Bram served. Paladins answer calls for aid against darkness.

## Publications

**Adventures Most Perilous** - B.B. Beauchamp's byline.

**The Solmara Gazette** - Major broadsheet published in Solmara. Prints news, notices, and Beauchamp's tales.

## Geography

**The West Coast** - Continent's western edge. Ocean to the west, mountains inland. Northern regions are wet, cold, and forested. Southern regions are warmer but still damp in winter.
